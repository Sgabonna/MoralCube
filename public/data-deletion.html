Your Right to Data Deletion

At The Moral Cube, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. In accordance with privacy regulations and our commitment to data protection, we provide this straightforward process for requesting the deletion of your personal information.

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data through any of the following methods:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to privacy@themoralcube.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Please include the email address associated with your account for verification purposes.

Send Email
Option 2: Account Settings

If you're logged in, you can delete your account and associated data directly from your account settings.

Navigate to your profile and look for the "Delete Account" option.

Option 3: Feedback Form

Submit a request through our feedback form with the category "Data Deletion Request".

We'll process your request and confirm completion via the email you provide.

Go to Feedback Form

For Facebook Users

If you connected to our service using Facebook Login, you can also:

  1. Visit your Facebook Apps and Websites settings
  2. Remove The Moral Cube from your list of connected applications
  3. This will prevent future data sharing, but to delete existing data, please also use one of the methods above

What Happens When You Request Deletion

When you submit a data deletion request, we will:

  1. Acknowledge receipt of your request within 7 days
  2. Verify your identity as the account owner
  3. Delete your personal information from our active systems, including:
    • Account information (name, email, profile picture)
    • Survey responses and analyses
    • Usage history and preferences
  4. Notify you once the deletion is complete (typically within 30 days)

Important Notes:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days before complete deletion
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked to you may be retained for research purposes
  • We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention

Questions About Data Deletion

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or need assistance, please contact us at privacy@themoralcube.com

For more information about how we collect, use, and protect your data, please review our Privacy Policy.
